DMG dealt scales off Albedo's DEF.·Transient Blossoms can only be generated once every 2s.·When a character is located at the locus of the Solar Isotoma, the Solar Isotoma will accumulate Geo power to form a crystallized platform that lifts the character up to a certain height.
