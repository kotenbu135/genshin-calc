## Passive Talents

### Protracted Prayers / 永き刻の祈り

Gains 25% more rewards when dispatched on an Inazuma Expedition for 20 hours.

**JA**: 稲妻で20時間かかる探索任務を完了した時、獲得する報酬+25%。

### Breaking Free / 檻を破る志

When Shinobu's HP is not higher than 50%, her Healing Bonus is increased by 15%.

**JA**: 久岐忍のHPが50%以下の時、与える治療効果+15%。

### Heart's Repose / 安心の処

Sanctifying Ring's abilities will be boosted based on Shinobu's Elemental Mastery:·Healing amount will be increased by 75% of Elemental Mastery.·DMG dealt is increased by 25% of Elemental Mastery.

**JA**: 久岐忍の元素熟知を基準に、越祓雷草の輪は以下の強化を得る。·アップする治療量：元素熟知の75%。·アップするダメージ量：元素熟知の25%。
